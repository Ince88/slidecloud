Company Data

Industrial heritage with second-generation leadership.

History

Founded by Mr. Béla Majoros in 1993. The company builds on more than 40 years of casting expertise and continues under the leadership of CEO Mr. Csaba Majoros.

Locations

Main plant in Békéscsaba with listed locations in Ajka, Apc and Szeghalom, creating a broader operational footprint in Hungary.
