Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to view a presentation?

No. Anyone with the link can view a presentation without signing up or logging in.

What file formats are supported?

You can upload PDF documents as well as JPG and PNG image files as individual slides.

How does the presenter remote work?

Scan the QR code on your phone. Your phone becomes a remote — swipe left or right to control the slides for all viewers in real time.

Can I update a presentation after publishing?

Yes. Load it back into the editor via "My Presentations", make your changes, and republish to the same link.
