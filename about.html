Our mission is to make presenting effortless

We believe great ideas deserve great delivery. Whether you're pitching to investors, teaching a class, or showing holiday photos to family — your presentation should be the star, not the software.

Flipcloud strips away all the complexity. Upload your slides, hit generate, and share a link. That's it. No accounts for viewers. No plugins. No friction.

And with the QR code presenter remote, you can control your slideshow from your pocket while your audience watches on their screens — in real time, from anywhere in the world.

Always available

Flipcloud runs 24/7 on globally distributed infrastructure. Your presentations are always just a link away.
